Featherdale Wildlife Park


This is a wildlife park located in Blacktown is an hours' drive away from downtown Sydney. Here you can get up close and person with koala bears, kangaroos & wallabies

Kiama

A 1hr 40mins drive from the Western Suburbs of Sydney will get you to the beautiful seaside town of Kiama. Here get to enjoy spectacular views of the South Pacific Ocean, an amazing blowhole and not forgetting to feast on outstandingly fresh seafood
